About Us

Becky Brooker

Becky is a physical therapist turned coder. She has lived in Iowa, Chicago, San Francisco, Denver, and Austin. Now Seattle is her newest home. No matter where she lives, the stars are shining above! Her lifetime love of space stems from family vacations as a child, star-gazing in the mountains, and nights spent partaking in astrophotography with her dad and dogs aka Astro and Laika.
